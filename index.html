What is Digital Assistant pro?

Digital Assistant Pro is a shopify plugin which helps the business owners to sell their digital products online with ease. These digital products can be ebooks, app subscriptions, giftcards, extended warranties, online courses, Software tools, Videos, Images, Audio books and clips, Music and ringtones, Graphics and digital arts, Documents templates, magazines etc.

How it works?
Signup

Just sign up from the website or directly from the shopify app store .

Sync

Sync your Shopify account with the DAP

Sell

Start selling your digital products with our feature loaded dashboard

What is Digital Order Fulfillment?

This helps Business owners to deliver their digital products to their customers with ease. Once the business owner has marked that the payment is received, Digital Fulfillment will jump on to work. It ensures that the product is delivered with all the necessary supporting documents so that the end consumer likes your business.Is also provides the necessary consumer support in case of any unforeseen circumstances.
